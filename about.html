About Emerald Article

Emerald Article is a scholarly publisher of academic journals and article in the fields of management, business, education, library studies, health care, and engineering.
The article content is available in PDF format that can be download and available for view and open access. Emerald Article is a free service where users can use and register.
Use the search bar to quickly locate specific academic article within the wide range available at Emerald Article.
